Ben Gibbard is a singer, songwriter and guitarist. He is the lead vocalist and guitarist of Death Cab for Cutie, with which he has recorded eight studio albums, and is one half of the electronic duo The Postal Service.
Gibbard released his debut solo album, Former Lives, in 2012, and a collaborative studio album, One Fast Move Or I'm Gone Music From Kerouac's Big Sur (2009), with Uncle Tupelo and Son Volt's Jay Farrar.
